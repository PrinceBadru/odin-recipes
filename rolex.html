ROLEX

Description

knead the dough of flour until it is not sticky any more ,cut the dough into balls of the same size

roll the balls they are flat and in a circular/sphere form ,fry them for like 5 minutes and then fry eggs and roll the eggs and chapati together with tomatoes in te middle

Ingredients

  • salt
  • water
  • flour
  • onions
  • eggs
  • tomatoes

steps

  1. knead the dough
  2. fry the chapati
  3. fry the eggs
  4. add the tomatoes into the egg and roll it
